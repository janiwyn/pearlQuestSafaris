    Day 2

    Gorilla Trekking Adventure

    Transfer from Entebbe to Bwindi impenetrable National Park

    You will be picked up by our guide, who will brief you about the whole safari before you travel to Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, which is found in the southwestern part of Uganda. Along the way, you will have a stopover at the Equator to take photos. Also, feel free to buy some crafts and souvenirs as you head to Mbarara town, where you will have your lunch. Thereafter, you'll proceed to Bwindi, where you will arrive in the late afternoon. You'll check in at the lodge, relax in the evening and have dinner.
