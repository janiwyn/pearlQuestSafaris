    Day 2

    Sipi Falls Experience

    Transfer to Kapchorwa for Sipi Falls Experience and Sabyini Culture

    You will be picked up very early in the morning by our tour guide and embark on your journey to Kapchorwa which will take 5-6 hours. Along the way, you will have a clear view of the Mabira forest, tea and sugar plantations. Expect to have a stopover in Mbale town for lunch and then proceed to Sipi Falls in Kapchorwa. You will go for a short hike at Sipi Falls and reach the top. While hiking the trail, you will pass through several plantations of local farmers and get a chance to learn about the local community and the local way of life. You will then head for a cultural experience with the Sebei people also known as the sabiny who are known for their trademark female genital mutilation and male circumcision. You will have an overnight at the lodge.

    Transfer to Kidepo Valley National Park for an Evening Game Drive

You will enjoy the slow drive as you take memorable pictures. You will then transfer back to the lodge for dinner and overnight.

    Day 4

    Game Drive & Cultural Experience

    You will enjoy your breakfast very early in the morning and head to experience a rewarding game drive through the open savannah, rocky outcrops, forests, and rings of mountains. You will drive extensively up to Kanangorok hot springs and have photo opportunities and chances to see more wildlife, especially lions. You will drive back to the lodge for lunch and relaxation. In the afternoon after lunch, you will embark on your journey to have a cultural experience with the Karamojong. This Community is still pure and unique from most other communities in Uganda. They have rejected the civilization and still live like in ancient times. They are very interesting group of people with a very unique culture visible in their way of dressing, cooking, dancing, and other daily life activities. It is a truly rewarding experience to visit the Karamojong. You will then drive back to the lodge for dinner and overnight.

    Day 3

    Morning Game Drive and Nature Walk

    You will do another game drive on the fourth day. You will have breakfast and then drive for your last game drive in the true wilderness. Every day presents a different sight you can expect to increase your game count. You will go back to the lodge for a hot lunch and after proceed with a nature walk where you will explore the savanna on foot with an armed ranger guide. Return to the lodge for dinner and relaxation and after have a sleep over.
